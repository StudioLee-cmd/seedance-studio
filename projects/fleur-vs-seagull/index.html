Fleur vs the Seagull

Tim Lee TV — 15 scenes, first+last frame method, ~60s
Fleur is eating Dutch fries on a terrace. She deliberately takes off her ring to eat without getting it greasy. She turns to the cafe for a second to ask about the parasol. The seagull strikes — eats the fries and swallows the ring. When she comes back everything is gone. The seagull taunts her with the ring on its claw, giving the middle finger. She snaps, grabs golf balls, and starts a full warfare attack. She destroys the whole terrace but finally hits the seagull, ring pops out. She catches it triumphantly — but gets arrested for the destruction. She's happy in the police car with her ring back. Seagull flies away free.
Status: Asset generation
Duration: ~63s
Format: 9:16 Shorts
Style: No dialogue, ambient sound only
